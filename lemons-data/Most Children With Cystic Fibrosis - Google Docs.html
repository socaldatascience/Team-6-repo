Children with Cystic Fibrosis
Lumacaftor/ivacaftor (Orkambi) is a prescription medicine used for the treatment of cystic fibrosis (CF) in patients aged 2 years and older who have two copies of the F508del mutation (F508del/F508del) in their CFTR gene.
